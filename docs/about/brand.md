# Brand Guidelines

**tuinix** represents technical excellence and terminal-first
computing. Our brand embodies warmth, community, and the power
of thoughtful minimalism.

## Mascot

Our mascot represents the spirit of tuinix:

- **Personality**: Wise, helpful, technically savvy, warm
- **Visual Style**: The character from our logo -- friendly face with characteristic hat
- **Usage**: Documentation, tutorials, error messages, community events
- Always friendly and approachable, never condescending

## Design Principles

1. **Community First**: We're stronger together
2. **Terminal First**: Clean, functional design respecting the command-line aesthetic
3. **Accessible Excellence**: High contrast, readable typography, inclusive design
4. **Technical Precision**: Every element serves the user's workflow
